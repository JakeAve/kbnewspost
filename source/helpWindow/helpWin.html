How to use the KBase News Post

Overview

The KBase News Post application allows users to send updates directly to the Knowledge Base. Use the following steps will help you successfully create a post to your News and Updates page.
  1. Create a Headline
    2. Identify the main message you want to communicate to your readers. Your headline should only be a few words long. The time and date will automatically generate as you type and before you post.
  2. Write Content
    3. Express your message in a few sentances. The tool bar at the bottom of the content box allows you to style text, create lists and insert other data. You can also right click your mouse to access the same tools. All of the tools, with the exception of the image button, work best when you highlight the piece of text you would like to edit and then choose a style. Because of this, it is easiest to write out all of your content before using tools.
    The buttons to the left allow you to bold, underline or change the color of text to red. To improve readability, these styles should be used sparingly (1 time per post) or not at all.
    The tool bar also has two list buttons. One creates an unordered list and the other creates a numbered list.
    The last two buttons allow you to create a link or add an image. Both of these require a full URL such as "https://www.youngliving.com/en_US" to function properly. Only use images when it is absolutely necessary.
  3. Choose icon(s)
    4. The category icons help readers quickly identify news posts. You should normally not use more than one or two icons.
  4. Send post to the Kbase
    5. The post should be sent within seconds. You will need to acknowledge your success or error message by clicking "OK."
  5. Preview update
    6. You will be able to preview the updated page directly in the application. The preview shows all of the content on your news page, including your new post. Select "Finish" to keep the post or use the "Undo" button to remove your most recent post. You can also select "View in Kbase" to see your live News and Updates page in a web browser.
  6. Finish
    7. After selecting "Finish", you will be prompted to either continue working on a post or close the application.
Please direct any additional questions or suggestions to a Kbase Administrator.
